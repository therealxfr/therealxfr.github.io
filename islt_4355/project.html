Internship Background

The Boeing Company

Actively studied military standards for government aircraft requests. Prepared Statements of Work, analyzed drawings, documents, and wiring diagrams for AWACS aircraft, and supported the LRU Qualification process, ensuring the testing and approval of devices used in aircraft for missions across different countries.

Commonwealth Edison

Gained an understanding of permit proposals, cross-section maps, manholes, and the differences between overhead and underground systems, as well as applications such as TED. Modeled a 26-mile underground fiber route, identifying the most efficient duct locations to optimize costs through cost/benefit analysis. Additionally, learned about transformers, fuses, and communication requirements to improve power distribution efficiency by up to 40%.

Wight & Co.

Gained insight into weekly preplanning and the ongoing construction management process, including coordinating with companies specializing in areas like glass and concrete. Participated in meetings with other companies involved in the project, learning about key safety regulations, site diversity, pricing discussions, work progress, efficiency, and critical deadlines that must be met.
